SECRET MUGS
OF THE SOUTH POLE

Ready to master the holidays one tip at a time? Click the BREW button below for a magical mug of holiday wisdom and be sure to leave a tip of your own! Who knows? Someone might just find yours somewhere near the South Pole…

The Secret Mugs of the South Pole were magically discovered on a cold, snowy night in an unknown location, which may or may not have actually been at the South Pole (and may have possibly been in the office of a downtown integrated marketing agency in Toronto). Filled with steaming cups of coffee and cocoa, the mugs revealed the secrets to a happy holiday season that had, to that point, even eluded some of the world’s greatest holiday minds. Somehow, while seeming simple and light-hearted, the words carried the wisdom of a hundred reasonably wise holiday guests.
